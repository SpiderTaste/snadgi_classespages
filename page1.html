My name is Sali Nadgi, I'm an aspiring artist based in the always flowing, Caribbean Island, of Puerto Rico. Where the people are nice, chillax, rough and you can find every mood under the bright sun. Needless to say, I love my Island. My love for all things art has been in me since I've had memory. From a young age I have bee in some art school, art program, in some private studio or just running around tabletop game shop painting minis, Warhammer 40k to be specific. I have my parents to thank for all my art adventuras, both of the creative in there own ways. Jumping at the moment they found an excuse to stick me into som artistic vernture. Love at first site.
